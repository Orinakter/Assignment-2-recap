Change Your Habit

Start small and set clear, achievable fitness goals. Establish a consistent workout routine and prioritize balanced nutrition. Stay hydrated and ensure you get 7-9 hours of sleep nightly. Find enjoyable physical activities, track your progress, and stay accountable to your goals. Be patient and persistent for lasting fitness results.

Time

Weight Loss

Combine regular exercise with a balanced, calorie-controlled diet, stay hydrated, prioritize protein, and practice consistency for effective weight loss.

Trainings and Exercises

Combine various training types for optimal fitness:
cardiovascular exercises (running, cycling), strength training (squats, deadlifts), flexibility work (yoga, stretching), balance training (single-leg stands), HIIT (burpees, sprints), and core exercises (planks, Russian twists). Incorporate low-impact workouts for recovery and active rest to enhance overall health.

BMI Calculator

To calculate BMI, divide your weight in kilograms by your height in meters squared. For pounds and inches, divide weight by height in inches squared and multiply by 703.
